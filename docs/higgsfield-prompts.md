# Higgsfield Render Pack — Samarth's Garage

Generate one image per car. **Keep the camera angle and lighting identical** across all
of them so the garage reads as one cohesive car-select screen. Save each output at the
exact filename shown (place in `portfolio/public/cars/`).

### Global settings (use for every car)
- Aspect ratio: **16:9** (landscape)
- Style keywords to append to every prompt:
  `photoreal, 8k, octane-render quality, high-end automotive advertising photography,
  Need for Speed Underground aesthetic, car centered, 3/4 front view, slight low angle,
  seamless dark background, no text, no watermark, no license plate`
- Lighting (same every time): `dark glossy wet reflective concrete floor, moody
  underground showroom, cyan key light + magenta fill neon rim lighting, volumetric haze,
  wet-look body reflections, shallow depth of field`

> Tip: for the 4 hero cars (BMW, Audi, Mahindra, Mercedes) you can *also* generate a
> **Higgsfield video**: append `seamless 360° turntable rotation, slow, 5-second loop` and
> save as `<slug>.mp4` — we'll spin those on their pedestals.
